Gabriette Bechtel (born 1997) is an American model, singer, and performer. She gained recognition as a member of the pop group Nasty Cherry, created under Charli XCX’s guidance. As a model, she has worked with international fashion brands and appeared in numerous campaigns. Her unique style and stage presence made her a rising figure in both fashion and music.

1997 – Present | Model and Music Artist

Important Life Events

  1. 1997 – Born in the United States
  2. 2019 – Joined the pop group Nasty Cherry
  3. 2019 – Appeared in the Netflix documentary series "I’m With the Band: Nasty Cherry"
  4. 2020 – Signed with major modeling agencies and worked with fashion brands
  5. 2022 – Became recognized for her unique personal style and stage presence
